The St John Bosco Camp is a week-long Catholic Summer camp designed for children aged 8-13. Bosco offers a unique opportunity to explore the outdoors, make new friends, and deepen campers faith through daily Mass and spiritual activities. Our experienced volunteers will guide campers through exciting outdoor activities like swimming, climbing, and team-building games, while providing a supportive environment for them to grow in their faith. It is out mission to make St John Bosco camp an incredible summer experience.

ABOUT

St. John Bosco was an Italian Catholic priest, educator, and founder of the Salesian Society. He dedicated his life to helping disadvantaged youth, particularly in the city of Turin, Italy during the 19th century. Bosco's approach to education was based on kindness, empathy, and understanding, and he emphasized the importance of practical training and vocational skills for young people. Throughout his life, Bosco faced many challenges and obstacles, including poverty, illness, and opposition from some members of the church. However, he remained steadfast in his commitment to his mission, and his work had a profound impact on the lives of countless young people. Bosco's legacy continues to inspire people around the world today, and he is remembered as a model of selflessness, compassion, and dedication to the betterment of society.

Our summer camp is inspired by the work and teachings of St. John Bosco! Bosco's mission is to provide a fun and engaging experience for children, while also instilling the values of kindness, empathy, and practical skills that were central to St. John Bosco's philosophy. Our volunteers are committed to creating a safe and supportive environment that fosters growth and encourages children to be their best selves. We believe that every child has the potential to thrive, and we are committed to helping them achieve their goals.

Who is eligible for camp?

Bosco welcomes both girls and boys aged between 8-13 years old.

When is camp?

We run 2 week-long camps in August.

Where is camp?

Bosco is located in Essex. Precise location will be provided after booking. We also organise coaches with pick up points in London and Essex.

Volunteer with us!

Who can volunteer?

Anyone over the age of 16+.
Volunteers will be required to complete a DBS and safeguarding training before the beginning of camp.

What does volunteering involve?

As a volunteer, you will be involved in a variety of different tasks including running activities, assisting with the cooking of camp meals and keeping the camp clean and tidy.
